Timeline

Coruscant has a rich history lasting over 100,000 years, showing the power of multiple different creatures, ultimately ending in the beautiful planet we have today!

  • Today

    After the re-formation of the Galactic Republic in the Ruusan Reformation in 1000 BBY, Coruscant lives on today striving with years of peace, and more to come!

  • 1100 BBY - Republic Dark Age

    The Jedi regained power over a Republic and Coruscant without a Republic senate, acting as the sole rulers.

  • 3653 BBY - Sith interference

    Plenty of wars occured between this and 1000 BBY momentariliy giving the Sith power over Coruscant and then back to more war stopping that.

  • 12,000 BBY - Galactic Museum secures a recorded history

    The grand Galactic Museum is formed, creating a safe place for history and its artifacts to reside for people of today to learn from.

  • 25,053 BBY - Galatic Republic foundation

    After the collapse of the Rakata rule, Coruscant signed itself off into the Galactic Constitution, forming the Galactic Republic, and setting Coruscant as the capital.

  • 30,000 BBY - Infinite Empire rule

    The Rakata took a liking to the central planet, and assumed ruler of Coruscant over the humans, leading a rule lasting ~5,000 years.

  • 90,000 BBY - Upward progression

    With the population rising, the first level was no longer good enough, more levels were created to facilitate more inhabitants.

  • 100,000 BBY - First city

    In 100,000 BBY Coruscant see's its first city, built by humans. These places are now left to ruin and buried beneathe the many new layers place atop them.
