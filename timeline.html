Timeline

Boruscant has a rich history lasting over 100,000 years, showing the power of multiple different creatures, ultimately ending in the beautiful planet we have today!


  • Today

    After the re-formation of the Democracy of the Universe in the Philin Reformation in 1000 BBY, Boruscant lives on today striving with years of peace, and more to come!

  • 1100 BBY - Democratic Dark Age

    The Triegh regained power over the Democracy of the Universe and Boruscant without the senate, acting as the sole rulers.

  • 3653 BBY - Malk interference

    Plenty of wars occured between this and 1000 BBY momentariliy giving the Malk power over Boruscant and then back to more war stopping that.

  • 12,000 BBY - Universal Museum secures a recorded history

    The grand Universal Museum is formed, creating a safe place for history and its artifacts to reside for people of today to learn from.

  • 25,053 BBY - Democracy of the Universe foundation

    After the collapse of the Rachasta rule, Boruscant signed itself off into the Universal Constitution, forming the Democracy of the Universe, and setting Boruscant as the capital.

  • 30,000 BBY - Infinite Empire rule

    The Rachasta took a liking to the central planet, and assumed ruler of Boruscant over the humans, leading a rule lasting ~5,000 years.

  • 90,000 BBY - Upward progression

    With the population rising, the first level was no longer good enough, more levels were created to facilitate more inhabitants.

  • 100,000 BBY - First city

    In 100,000 BBY Boruscant see's its first city, built by humans. These places are now left to ruin and buried beneathe the many new layers place atop them.
